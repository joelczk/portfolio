A.T.A.S

  • Date: January 2020 - May 2020

A.T.A.S is a CLI to help students track their assignments and events, and to help them in managing their time. ATAS is catered for students who want to maximise their productivity and cultivate effective time management habits through a desktop application, and is optimised for users who prefer to work with the CLI.

LumiRandom

  • Date: January 2019 - May 2019

LumiRandom was implemented to emulate a student management system, as part of CS2102 module in NUS. The database of choice for this project is PostgresSQL, and the backend framework used is Flask.
